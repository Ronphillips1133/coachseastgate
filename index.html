Coach's EastGate is the place to relax, eat good food and just hang out with friends. Located in Lebanon, TN (Just off of I 40 and Hwy 109), Coach's Eastgate Grille offers a full menu of mouth watering, finger licking just down right deee-licous appetizers, entrees and desserts. Come in and enjoy a good meal in a comfortable, friendly atmosphere and check out the large array of sports memorabilia throughout the establishment.

DIRECTIONS

Coach's EastGate Grille

Coach's Eastgate Grille is conveniently located off I-40 at the Hwy 109 exit.


From Nashville

Head east on I-40 to the 2nd Hwy 109 exit (Exit 232-B).
Continue to the First stop light. Turn left at the light onto Eastgate Drive.
Coach's Eastgate grille is located 1/4 mile down on the left.

From Knoxville

Head west on I-40 to the Hwy 109 exit.
Turn right. Go to the first stoplight and turn left onto Eastgate Drive.
Coach's Eastgate Grille is located 1/4 mile down on the left.
